Our Story

AuMai Delights was born from a simple place—being a mom looking for fun, creative activities to do with my girls and share with our family. What started as sweet moments in the kitchen quickly turned into something more. With encouragement from those around me, I realized these desserts could become more than just a hobby—they could become a business.

Seeing the excitement on my girls' faces and the joy that each dessert brought to others inspired me to keep going. That same excitement is poured into every creation. From dessert shooters to custom treats, each item is made with care, creativity, and attention to detail.

At AuMai Delights, baking is about more than just sweets—it's about creating memorable experiences, bringing people together, and turning everyday moments into something special. What began as family time has grown into a passion-driven business dedicated to making desserts that look beautiful, taste amazing, and leave a lasting impression.
